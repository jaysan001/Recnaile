Our advanced delivery drone is engineered for high-altitude, long-range, and heavy-payload logistics operations.

About Us

Who We Are

Recnailce toward the future, Recnaile is committed to pioneering passenger drone technology, enabling a new era of efficient, sustainable, and accessible air mobility.

Our prototype drones leverage AI-driven navigation, precision engineering, and autonomous flight technology to deliver safe, cost-effective, and high-efficiency operations. Birth of Company

Founded by Jeeva Madhaiyan in response to India's logistics challenges, our company was born from a vision to revolutionize delivery through customized logistics drones.

Recognizing the limitations of traditional methods, we set out to harness drone technology to enhance efficiency, streamline last- mile delivery, and ensure timely transport of critical goods. With a commitment to innovation, we aim to reshape the future of logistics, providing tailored solutions that drive customer satisfaction and operational excellence.
